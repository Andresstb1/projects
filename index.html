About Me

Hello, I'm Andrés Sanchez-Toril Bastrygina, a fourth-year Engineering Physics researcher and student with a focus on quantum and photonic technologies. My academic background in quantum mechanics, quantum computing, nanotechnology, and photonics has provided me with a strong foundation in understanding and designing quantum systems.

I have gained hands-on experience through an internship at a nanofabrication pilot line, where I worked on designing and testing integrated photonic circuits. I also co-founded a student team dedicated to developing innovative solutions in healthcare technology.

Through specialized courses and online programs, I've enhanced my expertise in hybrid quantum-classical algorithms, cost optimization, and quantum circuit design. My current research explores advanced materials for quantum applications, with practical experience in simulation, modeling, and quantum algorithm implementation.

I am passionate about advancing quantum technology and continuously seek opportunities to learn and contribute to cutting-edge projects.

Quantum Computing Labs

Hands-on Jupyter notebooks to explore different quantum computing concepts.

  • Lab 1: Introduction to Quantum Computing - Fundamental concepts of quantum computing and first steps with qubits.
  • Lab 2: Single-Qubit Gates - Exploration of basic operations on a qubit, including X, Y, Z, and Hadamard gates.
  • Lab 3: Multi-Qubit Gates and Entanglement - Implementation of quantum gates for multiple qubits and the phenomenon of entanglement.
  • Lab 4: Quantum Circuits and Quantum Teleportation - Building more advanced quantum circuits and experimenting with quantum teleportation.

You can view and run these notebooks directly on GitHub or download them for local execution.

Quantum Computing code

A comprehensive quantum computing project focused on implementing quantum algorithms, error correction methods, and spin simulations. The structure includes separate folders for algorithms, error_correction, and simulations:

  • algorithms/ - Scripts for quantum algorithms (e.g., quantum_phase_estimation.py)
  • error_correction/ - Methods for implementing error correction (e.g., shor_error_correction.py)
  • simulations/ - Numerical simulations of spin states (e.g., spin_simulations.py)
